Welcome to the official Delectable Desserts website! My name is Ary Peralta and i’ve been running my own bakery business for four years now. When I was ten years old, I fell in love with baking, and have been working to increase my skills and experience ever since. I have worked as an intern at Front Street Bakery in Rockville Centre and have worked for Mario's Bakery in Oceanside, which has only expanded my creativity and knowledge that I can put into my clients work to make something special for them. I specialize in making traditional dominican cakes, but also any dessert for occasions such as birthday parties, sweet 16's, and any holiday.

The Process

I take pride in the creative ideas I put into creating my custom cakes. For each one of my client's cakes, I plan my ideas for days to make sure it is something extra special and unique using only the finest ingredients. When working with me, I make the process of placing an order as simple and stress-free as I can make it. Here’s how simple it is :

  1. Plan a time and place to meet
  2. Discuss possible ideas of what the cake looks like and what the theme is
  3. I create a hand drawn picture of the cake, and make changes as needed to fit the client's needs
  4. Have the cake made for pickup in time for the occasion
